About me

I received the B.S. degree in applied mathematics from Fuzhou University (FZU) in 2013. In April 2018, I received the PhD degree in applied mathematics under the supervision of Prof. Wenxing Zhu at Fuzhou University (FZU). He is currently a Associate Professor at the School of Mathematics and Statistics, Minnan Normal University, Zhangzhou, China. He has authored over 20 papers in refereed journals and conferences, including TC, TVLSI, TODAES, INTEGRETION, ICCAD, ASPDAC, ISPD, ISVLSI. His current research interests include VLSI physical design and design for manufacturability, and graph neural network. Dr. Li was recipients of the First Place in the ICCAD 2017 Contest and the First Place in the ICCAD 2018 Contest, He received Excellent Doctor Degree Dissertation Award in Fujian and Graduate National Scholarship.
